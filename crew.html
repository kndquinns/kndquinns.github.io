Commander

Kevin Quinns

I am a private tutor and have a Masters degree in Science from Yale University, CT. I used to teach in a large school but then I quit that to start working independently to achieve my goals and I was soon rewarded with a much better position than before. I have all sorts of tutoring experience; teaching students from elementary school all the way up to year 12, homeschooled and non-homeschooled, as well as college and university students and have had wonderful experiences teaching those students all these years.

WHY I CHOSE TO TEACH

I grow at seeing my students succeed. I cherish helping them up, watching them and aiding them to grow. The immense sense of fulfillment I attain from helping other humans is why I chose this line of work and continue it with a passion even today.
