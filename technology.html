STRATEGY

I use specific strategic methods that best suit each students individual learning style and thus helps with resolving their personal weaknesses. Letting them bloom by getting the student excited about the subject and letting the students enthusiasm towards science and math sprout so they take the matter in hand. I analyze tough subjects and complex formulas into a simple, understandable form and as students spend more time with me, they gain that ability themselves.

EXPERIENCE

As a private tutor, I have experience handling even the most difficult subjects like math, physics and genetics which are parts of science where students are usually the weakest. I am an experienced teacher who has taught over 2500+ students during the past years. Most of these students had no knowledge whatsoever of Science or Math and did not know the basics of Mathematics or Science. I have taught hundreds of hours of homeschooled and non-homeschooled students and continue teaching and helping students achieve their future goals even now.

DETERMINATION

I assist my students to remove their barriers such as illiteracy and replacing that barrier with their own perception and understanding. Nothing is impossible, you just have to work through the problem no matter what it is or how hard it is. From a difficult math exam, to a complex physics formula, to a misunderstanding about molecular biology, you just have to work through it until you get to the core. I believe the key to all this is dedication. There is no reason a student should dislike a specific science unless it has been demonstrated wrongly.
